The Yeovil Pizza Company is a family-owned and operated pizzeria located in the heart of Yeovil, a bustling town in the southwest of England. Founded in 2010 by the Jones family, the company has quickly become a local favorite for its delicious and authentic pizzas made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

The menu at Yeovil Pizza Company features a variety of classic and specialty pizzas, including vegetarian and gluten-free options, as well as a selection of appetizers, salads, and desserts. All of the pizzas are made to order and cooked in a wood-fired oven, giving them a crispy, flavorful crust.

In addition to its dine-in service, Yeovil Pizza Company also offers takeout and delivery options, making it a convenient choice for busy families and professionals. The company prides itself on its friendly and attentive staff, who provide excellent customer service and ensure that every guest leaves satisfied.

Overall, Yeovil Pizza Company is a vibrant and welcoming restaurant that serves up some of the best pizza in town. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a relaxed evening out, this family-friendly establishment is sure to please.
